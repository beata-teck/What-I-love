About Books

Books are my quiet refuge—soft-spined worlds where time slows and thoughts deepen.
I love the scent of old pages, the hush between chapters, and the way a single sentence can linger like a melody.
Whether it’s poetry, fiction, or folklore, I read not to escape, but to feel more deeply connected to everything.

Here Are Some of my Favorite Authors

Anna Huang

Ana Huang’s books are the perfect mix of slow-burn romance, emotional depth, and addictive drama.
Her characters are flawed, fierce, and unforgettable—each story pulls you in and doesn’t let go.

Nita Prose

Nita Prose crafts clever, heartfelt mysteries with quirky characters and emotional depth.
Her breakout novel The Maid is a charming whodunit told through the eyes of an unforgettable protagonist.

Sydney Sheldon

Sidney Sheldon was a master of suspense and drama, known for fast-paced thrillers with glamorous settings, strong heroines, and jaw-dropping twists.
His iconic novels like If Tomorrow Comes and Master of the Game remain timeless favorites

Tahereh Mafi

Tahereh Mafi writes emotionally charged YA fiction with lyrical prose and unforgettable characters.
Her Shatter Me series blends dystopian drama with romance and resilience, captivating readers worldwide
